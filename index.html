Who we are?

People who survive complex and difficult life challenges

I am a BACP Registered Accredited Counsellor and Psychotherapist with over 20 years experience and offer both short and long term counseling. My work is bound by the Ethical Framework and Practice of BACP. In addiction to many years experience in private practice I have counseled in a GP practice as well as in various agencies one of which specialised in working with clients who had been bereaved and another with clients who had been abused in childhood or adulthood. My counseling approach is integrative. I also have a Diploma in CBT, NLP and Life Coaching. I see individuals and Couples and young people over the age of 15. In addiction I am a BACP Senior Accredited Supervisor with 12 years experience of working with both experienced and trainee counselors.
